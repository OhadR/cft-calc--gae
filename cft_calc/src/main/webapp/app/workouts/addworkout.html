How to enter a workout:

choose the workout from the dropdown list. if this is a repition-based WOD (e.g. cindy), the result is the number of repititions. For example, if you did "cindy", 10 rounds plus 4 pullups. in this case, the result is the number of total repititions: 10 rounds * 30 (5 pullups, 10 pushups and 15 squats) + 4 = 304.

If it is time-based WOD, the result in format of (minutes):(seconds), for example 01:12.

In the PR results, such as dead-lift, the result in kilograms, where 1 lb = 0.45 kg.
